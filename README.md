Transform any image into pixel art directly in your browser! This web app lets you upload an image, adjust the pixel size, and download your pixelated masterpiece.

---

## Features

- Upload any image (JPG, PNG, GIF, etc.)
- Adjust pixel size with a slider for a custom pixelated look
- Preview your pixel art in real-time
- Download the final pixel art as a PNG
- Fully client-side (no uploads to a server)

---

## Demo

You can try it directly in your browser by opening the `index.html` file.

---

## Usage

1. Clone the repository or download the ZIP.
2. Open `index.html` in your browser.
3. Click **Upload Image** and choose an image from your device.
4. Adjust the **Pixel Size** slider to your liking.
5. Click **Download Pixel Art** to save your creation.
